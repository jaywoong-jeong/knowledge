## Schedule

### Basics (Tabular MDP)
1. Intro, MDP basics and planning
2. Concentration inequalities
3. Generative models, value iteration
4. Online RL, exploration, optimism
5. Offline RL, pessimism
6. Minimax lower bound

### Advanced Topics
7. Policy optimization
8. Large state space, linear function approximation
9. General function approximation
10. Game theory and multiagent RL
11. Learning Markov games
12. Partial observable MDP
